GLASS 2015

A premium event of INVICTUS-15, organized by VIT Business School.

VIT GLASS is a string of simulated committees held for the benefit of aspiring leaders. The Global Leadership and Strategic Summit 2015 will bring together young leaders from all parts of the nation arrive to portray the role of various countries that comprise the United Nations. The delegates representing each Nation in a simulation format will aim to debate, scrutinise and bring about the needed transformation to more sustainable and inclusive decisions on various agendas in their respective councils.

This year GLASS'15 focuses the following councils: World Trade Organization (WTO), U.N. General Assembly first committee: Disarmament and International Security Summit (UNGA:DISEC), G20 (Group of twenty)

Invictus '15

A premium event of VIT Business School.

VIT Business School once again proudly introduces the international business and management fest, INVICTUS-15. Aimed to be organized at VIT University Vellore campus, INVICTUS-15 is a three days business oriented and intellectually challenging fest, custom made for the youth of the Nation. Providing a suitable environment to all business experts and novices, the fest plays host to a large footfall of students, faculty members and other eminent guests that arrive to honor the event. It is an amassment of a wide range of events from international conferences to flagship events and premium events.

VIT Business School

Established in 1994, at VIT Business School we are dedicated to nurturing excellence in our students and to shape them into future business leaders, who can bring a difference not only to their organizations but to their society as well. Over the years VIT Business School has developed itself into an institute imparting high quality management education through a system that emphasizes on "Building business ready Managers".

The institute has a vibrant campus life that offers a wide range of social, cultural and intellectual opportunities to help students to reach their potential. Step inside VIT Business School and you will find a whole new world filled with opportunity and resources waiting to welcome you.
